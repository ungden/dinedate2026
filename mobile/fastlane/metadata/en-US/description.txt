DineDate is an anonymous dating app combined with unique restaurant discovery in Vietnam.

How it works:
1. Create a date order at a partner restaurant, choose a combo and time.
2. Others apply to your date order — everyone is anonymous with DiceBear anime avatars.
3. Choose the right match, meet at the restaurant.
4. After the date, both rate each other. If both want to meet again — real identities are revealed!

Key features:
• Anonymous dating — safe, no pressure
• Discover partner restaurants with combo deals
• Rate restaurants on 4 criteria: food, ambiance, service, overall
• Integrated e-wallet — auto QR bank top-up
• Real connections based on real experiences, no swiping
• VIP/SVIP with priority display and special features

DineDate — Great food, memorable dates.
